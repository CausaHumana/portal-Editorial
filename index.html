Grupo da Causa Humana

A group of studies focussed on scientific and social causes

Grupo da Causa Humana is a group idealized by researchers, professor and friends, which study and discuss ideas in a health, peaceful and sustainable environment. Different contributions are achieving from a diverse group, composed of people with different backgrounds, goals, dreams and personal beliefs.
The city of Ouro Preto, Minas Gerais, Brazil, is the hearth of the foundation of our team.
